Here's a recap of the latest iOS projects I have worked on.

Nuclitopes

A scientific app written in both Swift 3 and Objective-C. This application contains a collection of information pertaining to each distinct nuclide, a calculator for nuclear quantities, a unit converter, and a community for asking and seeking all things nuclear.

An elegant interface for displaying the chart of nuclides.

A calculator for binding energy / separation energies

A handy unit converter

Access to "The Community", a place to ask and seek all things nuclear!

Nuke Defense

An iOS gaming app written entirely in Swift and developed using UIKit and SpriteKit

War has begun! The enemy has launched their nuclear weapon stockpile and it is up to you to defend your nation. Shoot and destroy the incoming missiles before they detonate and wipe out all in existence! No one can survive the nuclear fireball that forms at the center of the nuclear blast... But don't shoot the birds! No need to slay those innocent critters
